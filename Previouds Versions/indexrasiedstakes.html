A changing landscape

Transactional sales processes were designed to satisfy the traditional elements of value: can we guarantee the best price? Are these the right product specifications? Are we compliant with our customers’ ethical and regulatory standards?

With value elements shifting to include a much broader definition, the heavy reliance on manual inputs has made traditional processes very difficult to scale.

At Silverwing, we believe that growth comes not by making traditional processes faster and better, but from driving process innovation.

Our enterprise-grade digital infrastructure enables businesses to focus on selling rather than negotiating, helping them:

  • Provide flexibility (of choice and timing) to all customers in their buying processes
  • Complete transactions in a number of clicks, rather than a number of months
  • Strategically guide customers towards decisions that save money for all
